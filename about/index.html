About AirLytics

AirLytics is a Smart Community Health Monitoring and Early Warning System that predicts and alerts pollution-linked health risks in real-time. It collects air quality and health data to provide actionable alerts to communities and authorities.

Pollution-related diseases like asthma, lung infections, and cardiovascular problems are rising rapidly. AirLytics helps communities take preventive action in time, improves public health, and reduces hospital visits and medical costs.

  • Real-time air quality monitoring
  • Health alerts for pollution-linked diseases
  • Interactive dashboard with charts and maps
  • User-friendly interface
  • AI-powered predictive analytics for long-term trends
  • Integration with smart wearables for personalized monitoring
  • Mobile push notifications for community alerts
  • City-wide or country-wide deployment

Microservices architecture allows easy scaling. Cloud deployment enables large-scale data processing. The system can expand to multiple cities or regions efficiently.

  • Early warnings reduce health risks
  • Data-driven insights improve policymaking
  • Preventive care reduces healthcare costs
  • Increases community awareness for better public health
